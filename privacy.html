Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you register for our courses, including:

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe/PayPal)
  • Course progress and project submissions
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us:

  • Provide and improve our courses
  • Process payments securely
  • Offer personalized support
  • Send important updates (opt-out available)

3. Data Sharing

We only share data when necessary:

  • Instructors: Access your progress to provide feedback
  • Payment Processors: Stripe/PayPal handle transactions (we never store credit card details)
  • Employers: Only with your explicit consent for job placements

4. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Ask for corrections or deletions
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)

5. Security Measures

We protect your data with:

  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited staff access
